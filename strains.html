Strains


The cannabis plant is a genus of flowering plants in the family of cannabaceae. It is generally accepted that there are two dominant strains of canabis: sativa and indica, with a third being a hybrid of the two. Please read below to find out more information about canabis strains.

—

Sativa

The sativa strain tends to be an uplifting, happy high, with minimal body feel.

Indica

The indica strain tends to be a full-body, relaxing high. The indica strain will affect physical abilities more than mental ones, unlike sativa

Hybrid

The hybrid strain is an intermediate between the other two strains. It can provide both an uplifting mind feel, with a relaxing body feel. However, these strains lean towards either being a sativa dominant or indica dominant.
